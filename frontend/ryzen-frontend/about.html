ABOUT ME

Logic, Leadership,
and Manual Exposure.

Approaching tasks with the same clean architecture used in writing code.


Currently, I’m a Computer Science student at Ateneo de Davao University, where I serve as the Class President for my cohort. Managing communications, logistics, and schedules for a large group has hardwired me to be hyper-organized and proactive.


I don't just stick to theory. Recently, I led the development of AgriVysor, an offline AI crop disease detector for Android, taking a complex technical project from concept to a shipped product.


When I'm not untangling technical workflows or building web applications, I'm dialing in the manual exposure on my vintage Minolta SRT 101, pulling a shot of specialty coffee, or whisking my chasen with a ceremonial matacha. I do also like singing.


I bring that same focus, creativity, and rhythm to the businesses I support.
